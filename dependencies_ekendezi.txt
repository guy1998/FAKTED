• In order to print the bill contract, the car rental agent must enter the information of the customer and the available car.
• If a car is not available, the car rental agent cannot make a reservation for it.
• In order to make a rental reservation, the customer needs to log in, pick an available car and pay for the provided service.
• If a chosen car is not currently available, the customer may choose another car for reservation.
• In order to finalize the return of the vehicle, the customer must pay every fine placed on the vehicle.
• Customers are dependent on their subscription so that they can pick a certain car model.
• The customer cannot pick any car they want if they do not have a subscription on the service.
• In order to receive a discount on the next rental, the customer must have made reservations at least 5 times.
• A car cannot be available for reservations, if the driver has confirmed it is under use by another customer.
• The car’s availability is dependent on the condition of the car post-rental, checked by the driver, in order to be available for another time. 
• Customers cannot make reservations if they have not given the car rental agent all their required information.
• Customers cannot make reservations if they have not confirmed their payment for the service
